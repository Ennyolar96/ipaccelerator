Marketleag Launches IP Development Incentive for Africans

Incentive Description

This is an incentive program provided to support individuals and entities in Africa who have new/existing/improving product ideas or inventions, creations, designs and brands that could be developed and perfected as codified intellectual property assets such as patents, trademarks, industrial designs, and utility models.

The program would enable the participants to develop and expand the rights and also provide opportunities to leverage such rights to their full potential.

Benefits

All successful Applicants would:

  • Participate in free eight (8) weeks IP Development Workshop;
  • Enjoy reduction of about 40-50% in the cost of protecting the IPRs in the local or foreign economy (depending on the nature/type of the asset);
  • Have opportunity to access to our commercial line to exploit the assets commercially.

Eligibility

  • Applicants must be Africans who are individuals and entities providing STEM (Sciences, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) related solution to meet market or social need, creators and designers; including game designs, industrial designs, etc., SMEs and Startups.
  • They must have at least one unregistered assets in their possession, such as:
    • Product idea or invention that solves problems in the local economy and which could be scaled for export markets;
    • Logo that is connected to a product, brand, or company;
    • Design in terms of shape of object that is capable of application in the industrial domain or otherwise.
  • Application is not restricted to any age group or range.

Process

Application period: April 13, 2022 to May 13, 2022

Application processing: May 14, 2022 to May 31, 2022

IP Development Workshop: June 3, 2022 to June 24, 2022

Assets filling and registration, commences on July 1, 2022

Access to commercial line commences continues round the years

Share of incentive for Protection

Patents and Utility models 50%

Trademarks 30%

Industrial designs 20%
